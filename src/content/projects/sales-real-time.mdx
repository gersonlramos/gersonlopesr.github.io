## Problem

Without a consolidated view, tracking pet-product sales KPIs, payment-type mix, and per-seller performance requires manual spreadsheet work pulled from multiple transactional exports. That kind of ad hoc reporting doesn't scale and makes it hard for decision-makers to spot trends in real time.

## Approach

A simulated PostgreSQL transactional database models the sales schema (orders, sellers, payment methods) and feeds a Power BI report. The report surfaces KPI cards, a payment-type distribution chart, and a per-seller ranking table, giving a single place to monitor sales health at a glance.

## Impact

This reconstructs, end-to-end and on non-confidential simulated data, the same executive KPI dashboard pattern already disclosed as delivered in production at Pet Premium Distribuidora — publicly demonstrating the skill without exposing real sales data. The goal isn't a specific revenue figure; it's showing the full pipeline (schema design through report delivery) that supports that kind of executive decision-making.
